ElementCraft respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

ElementCraft does not collect any personal information.

  • No account sign-up is required.
  • No email address, name, or contact information is requested.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking is used.
  • The app stores minimal gameplay data locally on your device (e.g., unlocked elements, achievements, and progress).

2. How We Use Information

All gameplay data is used only by the app to:

  • Track your crafting progress
  • Unlock elements, compounds, and achievements

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your information to anyone.

4. Children's Privacy

ElementCraft is designed to be safe for all ages, including children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Internet Connection

The app works fully offline. Internet access is only used for:

  • Downloading the app from the App Store
  • Optional future content updates

6. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored by us, there is no personal information to access, update, or delete. All gameplay data can be removed by deleting the app from your device.
